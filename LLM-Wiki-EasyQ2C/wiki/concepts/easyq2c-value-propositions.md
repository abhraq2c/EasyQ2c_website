# EasyQ2C value propositions

## What it is

The three published differentiators on the EasyQ2C homepage explaining why specialized Q2C expertise beats a general software agency.[^home]

## Description

Introduced under **"Why Choose EasyQ2C?"** — framed as Quote2Cash specialization that affects the client's bottom line.[^home]

### Deep Domain Expertise

EasyQ2C speaks "contracts, revenue recognition, and renewals" — not just code. Business logic understanding is embedded in every line of software.[^home]

### Scalability First

Solutions designed to handle the first 100 customers as smoothly as the next 10,000 — future-proof architecture from the start.[^home]

### Outcome-Driven

Success measured by **time saved** and **speed of revenue recognition**, not by features shipped.[^home]

## Variants

These three pillars are the only value props explicitly sourced from the homepage. Additional messaging (tagline, mission) lives in separate concept/brand pages.
