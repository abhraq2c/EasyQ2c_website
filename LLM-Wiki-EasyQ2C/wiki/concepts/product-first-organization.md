# Product-first organization

## What it is

EasyQ2C's strategic evolution from software consultancy to a product-first organization — building scalable, reusable software products rather than only delivering bespoke consulting engagements.[^home]

## Description

After years in software consultancy, EasyQ2C identified recurring client pain points (revenue leakage, slow collections) and shifted to building **scalable, high-performance software** that addresses those problems directly.[^home]

This framing supports the company's dual model:
- **Core products** — Debt Collection Systems, Spend Tracker, SubsSpend
- **Services** — custom engineering and integration for clients needing proprietary solutions[^home]
