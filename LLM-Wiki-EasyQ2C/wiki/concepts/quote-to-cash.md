# Quote-to-Cash (Q2C)

## What it is

Quote-to-Cash (Q2C) is the business domain spanning the lifecycle from quoting and contracting through revenue recognition, billing, collections, and renewals — EasyQ2C's core area of specialized expertise.[^home][^logo]

## Description

EasyQ2C positions Q2C expertise as its key differentiator against general software agencies.[^home] The company speaks "contracts, revenue recognition, and renewals" — not just code.[^home]

The brand tagline **"Making Quote to Cash Easy"** (see [Making Quote to Cash Easy](./making-quote-to-cash-easy.md)) explicitly names this domain and the company's value proposition within it.[^logo]

### Problems addressed in Q2C context

- Untracked revenue leakage[^home]
- Slow collection cycles[^home]

### Products and services in this domain

- [Debt Collection Systems](../entities/debt-collection-systems.md) — collections and revenue recovery
- Enterprise platforms automating financial business logic[^home]
