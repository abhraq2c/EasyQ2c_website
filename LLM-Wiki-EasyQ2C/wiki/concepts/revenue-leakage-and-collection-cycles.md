# Revenue leakage and collection cycles

## What it is

The pair of "silent profit-killers" that motivated EasyQ2C's product-first pivot: **untracked revenue leakage** and **slow collection cycles**.[^home]

## Description

EasyQ2C identifies these as common problems across companies in the software consultancy space — not isolated client issues but systemic gaps that scalable software can address.[^home]

The company's mission to ensure **no money is left behind** directly responds to these problems.[^home] [Debt Collection Systems](../entities/debt-collection-systems.md) targets the collection-cycle side; enterprise automation addresses leakage through better business-logic enforcement.[^home]
