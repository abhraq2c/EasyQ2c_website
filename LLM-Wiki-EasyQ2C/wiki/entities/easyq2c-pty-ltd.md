# EasyQ2C Pty Ltd

## What it is

EasyQ2C Pty Ltd is an Australian IT software and services company that builds high-performance software products focused on Quote-to-Cash (Q2C) workflows, revenue recovery, and business process automation.[^home]

## Properties

- **Type:** company
- **Owner / Operator:** EasyQ2C Pty Ltd
- **Status in our context:** in-use

## Description

### Origin and positioning

EasyQ2C began in software consultancy and evolved into a **product-first organization** after recognizing recurring client problems: untracked revenue leakage and slow collection cycles.[^home] Rather than only identifying bottlenecks, the company builds tools to eliminate them.[^home]

### Mission

The stated mission is to ensure **no money is left behind**, grounded in deep financial logic and modern application development.[^home]

### Differentiation

EasyQ2C positions itself against general software agencies through specialized **Quote-to-Cash** expertise — understanding contracts, revenue recognition, and renewals, not just code.[^home]
